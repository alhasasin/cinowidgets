Our Story

CINO Bakery & Confectionary was founded with one simple goal — to bring smiles to every home through fresh, warm, and delicious baked goods. From our humble beginnings in Zaria, we’ve grown into a trusted local favorite for families, students, and bread lovers.

Every loaf we bake and every snack we make is crafted with passion, using quality ingredients and time-tested recipes. We pride ourselves on combining traditional baking techniques with modern innovation to create irresistible tastes.

Our Mission

To deliver fresh, healthy, and affordable baked goods that make every customer’s day a little sweeter.

Our Vision

To become the most loved bakery in Nigeria, known for quality, consistency, and community connection.
